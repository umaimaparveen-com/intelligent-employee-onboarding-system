# IT Setup Guide

## Laptop Setup
1. Power on your laptop and connect to Amiseq-Guest WiFi
2. Contact IT at it-support@amiseq.com to get onboarded to Amiseq-Corp WiFi
3. Install Jamf Connect for device management (link sent via email)
4. Enable FileVault encryption — required within 24 hours of receiving laptop

## VPN Setup
1. Download Cisco AnyConnect from the IT portal: it.amiseq.com
2. Enter server address: vpn.amiseq.com
3. Use your company email and temporary password (sent by IT)
4. Complete MFA setup via Microsoft Authenticator
5. Contact IT if you face issues: it-support@amiseq.com or Slack #it-help

## Email & Calendar
- Email is hosted on Google Workspace
- Your email format is: firstname.lastname@amiseq.com
- Calendar invites for onboarding sessions will be sent on Day 1

## Software Access
- GitHub: Request access via #it-help Slack channel
- Jira: Auto-provisioned within 24 hours of start date
- Notion: Invite sent to company email on Day 1
- Figma: Request via your team lead

## Security
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters
- MFA is mandatory for all systems
- Never share credentials with anyone including IT staff
