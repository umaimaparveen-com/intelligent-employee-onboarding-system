# Frequently Asked Questions

## Q: What is the payroll date?
A: Salaries are paid on the 25th of every month. If it falls on a weekend, payment is made the Friday before.

## Q: How do I access the VPN?
A: Download Cisco AnyConnect from it.amiseq.com and use your company credentials with MFA.

## Q: What is the dress code?
A: Dubai and London offices follow smart casual. Bangalore is casual. Business formal is required for client meetings.

## Q: How do I submit expenses?
A: Use the Expensify portal. Submit within 30 days. Receipts required for amounts above $25.

## Q: Who do I contact for IT issues?
A: Email it-support@amiseq.com or message the #it-help Slack channel.

## Q: How many days of annual leave do I get?
A: 20 days of annual leave per year, plus public holidays based on your office location.

## Q: How do I get badge access?
A: Badge access is set up by the office admin on your first day. Contact admin@amiseq.com if you have issues.

## Q: Where can I find my payslip?
A: Payslips are available on the HiBob portal at hibob.amiseq.com.

## Q: Can I work remotely?
A: Yes, up to 3 days per week after your first 30 days, with manager approval.

## Q: Who is my buddy?
A: Your onboarding buddy will be assigned and introduced via email before your start date.
