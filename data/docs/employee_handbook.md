# Amiseq Employee Handbook

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
All remote employees must be available during core hours: 10am–4pm local time.
Remote work is not permitted during the first 30 days of employment.

## Leave Policy
- Annual Leave: 20 days per year
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Maternity/Paternity Leave: 16 weeks paid
- Public Holidays: As per local office location

## Expense Policy
Expenses must be submitted within 30 days via the Expensify portal.
Meals: Up to $50/day when traveling. Receipts required for anything above $25.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to treat colleagues with respect.
Harassment of any kind will result in immediate disciplinary action.

## Dress Code
- Dubai & London offices: Smart casual
- Bangalore office: Casual
- Client meetings: Business formal regardless of location

## Payroll
Salaries are processed on the 25th of every month.
If the 25th falls on a weekend, payment is made the preceding Friday.
Payslips are available via the HiBob portal.
